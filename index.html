Take Action

"what can i do?"

Now you might be wondering what you can do to help, and that makes us really happy!

This is a big project with even bigger dreams so it might seem impossible, but trust us, it's easier to help out then you think!

We have a monthly donation system for those who want to help, but might be unable to find the time, plus, our monthly donations are 'pay-what-you-can' and go as low a just 2$ pr month.

Even just spreading the word helps us too!

donations

Becoming a part of Project Wooden Steps is very easy, just 'Sign Up' and you'll be able to sign up for our monthly subscription as well as one time donations. You even get an overview of how many plants you have planted with your donations!

If you sign up for our newsletter as well we'll promise not to fill your inbox with waves of meaningless things, we strive to only send out newsletters when there are actual news to spread.

And trust us, you are going to want to stay alert for the things we have planned!

About Us

who are we?

We are a bunch of people who got sick and tired of of seeing no-one do anything, so we decided to do something about it!

We are a tight-nit team of dedicated and experienced people looking to create a better future for those coming after us, and we believe we have both the knowledge and understanding to make that dream a reality.

And with your help, our goals are just over the horizon!

our vision

We want to bring nature back into the cities, to the people, by reclaiming sites of torn down or abandoned buildings, and populating them with beautiful tress, flowers and various other plants.

As we strive to accomplish this internationally, we take great care when considering the climate and surroundings of the reclamation site as to ensure all plants will remain healthy and the site sustainable long after we're finished.

We consult countless biologists and various other plant specialists in making sure we do right by everyone.

funding

We are a nonprofit organization, thus 100% of your donations will go to the cause and we will be buying seeds with every last cent we get.

This ensures that we stay true to you as well as ourselves when working towards our future and that our actions are always motivated by the goals we set.

Rest assured we have a much greener world ahead of us!
